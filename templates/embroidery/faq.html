What's 3D Embroidery or 3D puff embroidery and regular Embroidery?
3D embroidery or 3D puff embroidery is a three-dimensional look to your embroidery design. 3D embroidery is a cool effect because it can make a design really stand out on a cap. 3D Embroidery would be raised and more thread and use for cap only. Regular embroidery Work out on every Appare.
